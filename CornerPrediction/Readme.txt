CornerNet  

Generates heatmap from the given image.Two heatmaps are produced one for the topLeft 
corner and one for the bottomRight.
Training data was generated by drawing a circle centred around each corner on the training set and
reducing the weight of the points as the get far from the centre.The radius of the circle is the harmonic
mean of height and width scaled by a constant

Tools :

Tensorflow
OpenCv
Padas
Anaconda package
